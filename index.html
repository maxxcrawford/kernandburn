What am I lookin’ at here?

KERN & BURN is Tulsa’s first annual, fast-paced design competition ignited by Art Directors Club of Tulsa. Designers, copywriters, misfits and creatives of all types will band together to crank out their best work under the tightest deadlines. In order to claim victory, cash money and bragging rights for a year, teams of 2-4 must fight their way through three fiery rounds of audience banter and fierce judgement, using only the design assets provided to them.

I’m a designer and you don’t scare me!

We must warn you, this is not for the faint of heart so be sure to check out the details below and bring your most supportive friends, because rivalries are born here. Number of spots are limited, so find your match and sign up fast.

Rules

  • Popular vote wins $50
  • Judges vote wins $150 and trophy
  • Teams must include 2-4 members, however roles and titles are completely up to you (copywriter, videographer, strategist, architect, photographer, drink runner, emotional support)
  • Laptops, computers, hard drives, headphones and any tools you may need during the competition are your responsibility
  • Design programs will not be accessible through WIFI
  • Your team will be required to have a flash drive or hard drive day of event for submitting work
  • You may only use the design assets provided to you for each round with a total of three rounds lasting 30 minutes (assets will be loaded to your computer or hard drive within an hour of the competition, but no peeking)
  • $60 registration fee per team / $50 registration fee per team with at least one ADCT member

This is lit, but I can’t creatively commit.

No problem! Attendance is free and open to everyone. All that we ask is you bring your most passionate self, your wildest friends and get ready to place your votes and raise some hell! All proceeds go directly to funding student scholarships and Art Directors Club of Tulsa.
